---
term: VPA
aka: [virtual payment address]
---

UPI's addressing layer: a human-readable handle (`name@bank`) that resolves inside the network to real account plumbing. The payer holds a stable name; the plumbing can change underneath without the payer noticing. DNS for money (cut-0201) — and the pattern agentic payments has yet to reproduce: a portable way to name who may pay whom, independent of rail.
