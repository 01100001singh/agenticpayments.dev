---
term: Two clocks
---

Every payment system runs an **experience clock** (what the payer watches: tap, spinner, ✓) and a **money clock** (what banks obey: clearing, netting, settlement finality, dispute windows). No mainstream rail synchronizes them; the craft is in hiding the gap. Whoever bridges the gap is extending credit and eating risk, named or not. The frame this whole site keeps returning to — see cut-0200 and both [Plates](/plates/).
