---
term: Mandate
---

A signed, machine-verifiable record that a principal authorized an agent to make a payment (or class of payments) under stated constraints — amount cap, scope, expiry. The mandate is the evidence layer of agentic payments: when a transaction is disputed, it converts "my agent did what?" from a customer-service scream into a claim that can be ruled on. See [The Mandatum](/bestiary/the-mandatum/) for the field guide.
